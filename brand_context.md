## Intelligence Bot Overview
An automated research and insight bot that monitors the health supplement and pre-workout space and posts structured intelligence reports to a Discord channel twice a week.

- **Schedule:** Every Monday & Thursday at 9:00 AM IST
- **Hosting:** Render (free tier)
- **Stack:** Node.js, Claude Sonnet 4 API with web search, Discord Webhook, node-cron
